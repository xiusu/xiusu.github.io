Xiu Su 苏修

Make it simple. Make it work.

I am currently a full professor at Central South University. I obtained my PhD from the University of Sydney, majoring in artificial intelligence. My supervisers are Chang Xu and Shan You. Before that, I received my B.S and M.S degree at Tianjin University, advised by Prof. Xiaodong Chen. I also join a exchange program at Nankai University during 09/2012-09/2014.

  xisu5992@uni.sydney.edu.au                               Google Scholar

Research Interest

My research interests include AutoML and other computer vision and machine learning topics, such as image classification, image inpainting, HOI, and object detection.
